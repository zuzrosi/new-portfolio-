Made.com

We were asked to identify the business drivers behind Made's desire for a native mobile application, identify the parts of the customers' (research, purchase, and ownership) experience which would benefit from a dedicated application and design a solution which aims to improve a key part of the experience.

Made.com is a furniture online retailer, that cuts out the middleman and links consumers directly with the designers. This allows the organisation to provide high quality, made to order, cool (contemporary) designed furniture straight to consumers at low prices. all transactions take place through their website which reduces waste and surplus stock.
