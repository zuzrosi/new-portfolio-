About me

I’m a UX designer. I recently finished UXDi course, that was a great chance to combine my design background with the practical approach of user experience.

I am a curious, motivated and creative person with an eye for clean design and details. My aim is to translate high-level requirements into beautiful, intuitive and highly-functional designs. I would like to join a company that values UX methodologies and is passionate about making people’s life easier by creating innovative and beautiful designs.
